PhD Student · Statistics

Jules
Bangard

I'm a PhD Student at the IRMA laboratory of the Université de Strasbourg, Statistics team, supervised by Étienne Birmelé. My thesis focuses on Statistical Algorithms for the detection of drug cocktails' adverse events. I'm broadly interested in statistical and machine learning methods with a focus on medical applications. I am the main developer of emcAdr, a C++ R package I built for detecting high-risk drug interactions from the FDA's FAERS dataset.
